    Scout Charter

    Helping boy scouts advance through life

    Welcome . . .

    Scout Charter is a website dedicated to helping boy scouts succeed in their scouting advancement. Its focus is to provide scouts, parents, and leaders with the simple tools they need to accomplish this endeavor.

    . . . Aboard

    To sign up, you'll need to provide your name and patrol number.

    Features

    Track advancement

    With a simple view of ranks and merit badges, scouts can quickly see their progress in advancement. Parents can too. Leaders can manage the ranks and merit badges scouts have earned.

    Set goals

    Scouts can set merit-badge goals to keep them focused on what scouting steps to take next.

    Find merit badge counselors

    When scouts set merit badge goals, they can view lists of merit badge counselors to help them pass off requirements.

    Request merit badge books

    Scouts can also request merit badge books. They will be informed if or when books are available. Leaders are given the responsibility to contact the scout after a book has been requested to make sure they receive it.

    Record notes

    Leaders can add notes/comments on their scouts' progress. This helps remind leaders the latest status on scouts and their advancement.
